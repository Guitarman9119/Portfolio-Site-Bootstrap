Nucleo Guitar Effects Pedal

The primary aim of this project is to design and construct a multi-effects pedal that can be reprogrammed for the electric guitar. The multi-effects pedal and guitar should be able to be reprogrammed using a computer or function without any other external accessories when just practicing. This functionality is important for the guitarist.

Makerspaceworld

Makerspaceworld is a website dedicated to STEM education. The site is for anyone who wants to join the makerspace revolution that use the principles of S.T.E.A.M (Science, Technology, Engineering, Arts and Mathematics). You will find resources, tutorials, lesson plans projects and so much more to help you on your journey whether you are a student/teacher/hacker.
